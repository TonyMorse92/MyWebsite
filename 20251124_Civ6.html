Civ 6 OG Leaders Challenge

Original posting date: 2025-11-24

I've played Civ 6 pretty much every week consistently for the past couple of years.
Despite the frequent play, and the even more frequent YouTube video binges,
I still haven't beaten the game on the highest difficult yet.

I don't think this is due to lack of ability, more a lack of seeing the games through to the end.
I'm not good enough to win any match I spawn into on the highest difficulty
(even ignoring the games where aggresive Civs spawn 15 tiles away),
but I do think I can win a plurality given that I don't restart the first time I feel slightly bored.

Now Civ 6 is a video game, whose primary utility is as a source of entertainment, so starting over when I get bored
(pretty much everyone agrees the early game, at least against the AI, is much more fun than the latter parts)
isn't the biggest of deals. However, I tend to have an issue comitting to a path and following through to the end in a lot of areas,
and I view my frequent restarts on Civ as emblematic of a larger problem.

Hence, I'm giving myself a little challenge. There were about 20 original Leader/Civilization combos when Civ 6 came out.
I'm going to win at least 1 game with each. The game modes and map settings will vary,
though I am going to refrain from the "meme" setups (e.g. Russia on a cold, wet map).

There's no hard timeline for this, though I'm expecting to be done by the end of 2026.
I'm cutting back on video games, but I figure I can conceivably get through 1 game
(which usually takes 4-8 hours on the shortest speed) fairly easily.
